Sanuki-Udon Imayuki

The udon at Sanuki Udon Imayuki is made with homemade, super-aged, multi-hydrated noodles. Depending on the type of udon, you can choose between "hiya-atsu" and "hiya-hiya". The image shows the Hiya Hiya "Kitsune Udon," and the large size of the kitsune is said to be very satisfying. The udon soup is their original soup made with a lot of Rishiri kelp.The image shows the cold "Bukkake Udon", with thin and firm noodles. The noodles are thin and firm, and are popular for their refreshing and elegant taste.

Okonomiyaki Tigusa

Okonomiyaki Senso" is an okonomiyaki restaurant located in Tenjinbashi-suji shopping arcade, a two-minute walk from Tenma Station. It is located in Tenjinbashi-suji Shopping Street, a two-minute walk from Tenma Station, and is adorned with a colorful paper lantern and a curtain with the restaurant's name on it. The restaurant is open for lunch from 11:00. The popular menu item, "Specialty Senso-yaki," is an original dish of pork loin sandwiched between okonomiyaki dough. This menu has been around since the establishment of the restaurant, and is characterized by the sprinkling of poppy seeds to finish it off.

Harukoma

Harukoma is a sushi restaurant with a long history, established in 1953. It is located in the Tenjinbashi-suji shopping district, a three-minute walk from Tenjinbashi-suji 6-chome Station. The restaurant has been loved by the locals for a long time. The restaurant opens for lunch at 11:00. Harukoma is said to have a wide selection of fresh sushi items. The order is written on a piece of paper. The "Kami Eel" is grilled to perfection, and the combination of rice, ingredients, and sauce is a perfect match.
