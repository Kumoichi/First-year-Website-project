Mount Fuji

Mount fuji is the highest mountain in Japan. It became the world heritage site. Since it becomes more popular more and more people visiting Mount fuji for their sightseeing. It is Located between Yamanashi and Shizuoka prefectures, this active volcano is loved by many people as a symbolic mountain of Japan. Mount fuji is one of the three most famous mountains in Japan and one of the 100 most famous mountains in Japan, and is a popular spot for climbers of all ages (season: July to August). There are four mountain trails (Fujinomiya route, Suzuru route, Gotemba route, Yoshida route), and since the roads and trails up to the fifth station are well maintained, many people visit by bus or car. If the weather is clear, it is a popular tourist spot with a panoramic view of Lake Yamanaka, Fujiyoshida City, and Lake Kawaguchi below.
