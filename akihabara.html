Akihabara is known as one of Japan's most popular electronics districts. Akihabara is a hub of subcultures with many stores related to games, manga, and anime, as well as many stores selling electrical and IT products. It is also known as a sacred place for "cosplayers" who dress up in costumes of popular idols and anime characters.
